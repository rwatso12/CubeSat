Attitude Control (MAI-200, MAI-400, passive, active, spinning, gravity gradient)

	Passive Magnetic Attitude Control

	The subsystem consists of a permanent bar magnet in combination with hysteresis rods.
        The bar magnet interacts with the local magnetic field to torque the spacecraft toward 
	the local magnetic field direction. As the spacecraft progresses in its orbit, the 
	local magnetic field induces a changing magnetic moment in the hysteresis rods.
